Students must develop a higher level of proficiency in shorthand writing to meet the industry's standards as medical transcription demands precision and speed. However, many students encounter challenges in mastering the subject. The researchers assessed the proficiency levels of fourth-year Bachelor of Science in Office Administration students in medical shorthand and determined the challenges they face in acquiring this skill. The findings served as a basis for the development of an E-learning Assessment Website to enhance their learning and proficiency.
